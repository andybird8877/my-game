# Daily Character Brainstorm — 2026-06-28

## Slot: evil · light · mage

### Why this slot?
All 14 non-named slots are available, but **evil / light / mage** is the most extreme unfilled combination and the most interesting design challenge. With the lowest possible HP pool and a mage's high SP damage, it's the game's only true glass cannon — and no current character occupies that archetype.

**Base stats (from game formulas):**
- HP: `200 × 0.75 = 150` (lowest in the game)
- AT: `9 − 1 = 8`
- SP: `18 − 1 = 17`
- Crit chance: `10%` (same as all light characters)

---

## Character Concept: **Sable**

*"She doesn't absorb the blow. She drinks it."*

A spectral dark mage who feeds on the damage she takes, converting enemy aggression into retaliatory SP power. Where Mourne self-harms deliberately to build force, Sable is harmed by the opponent — and turns it against them.

### Identity
- **Archetype:** Glass cannon / comeback engine
- **Fantasy:** A wraith-like sorceress who grows deadlier the more desperate her situation becomes
- **Tone:** Cold, predatory, unhurried — she lets you think you're winning

---

## Unique Mechanic: ECHO

When Sable **takes damage**, she stores a portion as **Echo charge**.
When she wins with **SP**, she fires her Echo charge as bonus damage on top of normal SP damage.

This creates a core tension: taking hits builds her power, but at 150 HP she can't absorb many before dying. Every turn she absorbs damage is a calculated gamble.

### Passive progression (unlock tree idea):

**RESONANCE** *(early unlock — after taking damage 3 times)*
Echo charge accumulates at 25% of damage received. Fires automatically on any SP win.

**REFRACTION** *(mid unlock — after 2 successful SP Echo bursts)*
Echo charge rate increases to 40%. Additionally, if Sable's SP wins on a **toggled good read**, the Echo fires at 1.5× multiplier.

**NULLFIELD** *(late unlock — after taking damage while at ≤ 30% HP)*
Once per match, when Sable drops to ≤ 30% HP, she gains a one-turn damage immunity (BL-equivalent effect that negates all incoming damage that turn, regardless of her move). This lets her survive long enough to fire her accumulated Echo.

---

## Ultimate: SHATTER

**Damage = total Echo absorbed across the entire match**

Like Mourne's Collapse, this rewards patient, deliberate play over the whole fight. Unlike Mourne, Sable has no heal on her ultimate — it's a pure glass-cannon payoff. The tradeoff: Mourne self-harms to build her stack; Sable needs the opponent to hit her, which means she can't turtle.

No lifesteal on SHATTER. Sable lives or dies by how well she times it.

---

## Play Style Summary

Sable rewards players who can tolerate being hit strategically. Her ideal gameplan:
1. Absorb a few hits early (building Echo, accepting that 150 HP won't last forever)
2. Win with SP to burst Echo damage and keep the opponent guessing
3. Save the ultimate for after Resonance/Refraction are online to maximize total Echo absorbed

Her counter: a player who can deal consistent small chip damage slowly drains her HP before Echo stacks matter. She's weak against Cairan (crit + bleed ticks) and strong against slow, heavy-hitting opponents (Mourne, heavy tanks) where each hit packs more Echo value.

## Name Notes
- **Sable** — simple, dark, gender-neutral, easy to remember
- Alternatives: *Vex* (taken by Cairan), *Nyx*, *Shade*, *Mirra*

## Art Direction Notes
- Light weight, mage class → visually thin/ethereal, no armor
- Evil affinity → dark color palette, maybe semi-transparent/ghostly silhouette
- Echo mechanic → visual idea of cracks of purple/black energy under her skin that glow brighter as she takes damage
